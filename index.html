About & Methodology

Purpose

A transparent, daily-updated registry of corporations that financially support, enable, or profit from: the Trump administration's agenda, Israeli military operations in Gaza, ICE detention, private equity extraction, environmental destruction, labor violations, surveillance, child exploitation, bipartisan corporate corruption, and disinformation.

Categories

Pro-Trump — supports Trump/Republican agenda. Pro-Israel — supports Israeli military/occupation. Both — both. Bipartisan — funds both parties to protect corporate interests. Global Harm — systemic harm not tied to one party.

Impact Score Rubric

4Significant: $1-2M donation, major defense/ICE contract, top BDS target, key legislation
3Moderate: <$1M donation, lobbying win, price gouging, deregulation benefit
2Indirect: PAC, minor lobbying, DEI rollback, peripheral ties
1Peripheral or emerging concern
