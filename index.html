About & Methodology

Purpose

Transparent, searchable registry of U.S. corporations identified as financially or politically supportive of Donald Trump, the State of Israel/Zionist causes, or both — grounded in verifiable public records. Tracks donations, government contracts, investments, lobbying, and BDS designations.

Impact Score Rubric

5≥$2M donated to Trump inauguration AND/OR direct weapons/major infrastructure contracts to Israeli military
4$1M–$2M Trump donation OR top BDS priority target OR major defense investment benefiting from admin agenda
3<$1M Trump donation OR moderate Israel ties OR significant lobbying win under Trump
2PAC/industry-association support, minor lobbying, or indirect investment ties
1Indirect or peripheral involvement; no disclosed direct political activity

Incident Types

Donation — Direct financial contributions to Trump campaigns or inauguration fund. Contract — Government or defense contracts awarded under Trump administration or weapons supplied to Israel. Investment — Financial ties, equity stakes, or economic activity that benefits from the administration's agenda. Lobbying — Registered lobbying activity supporting deregulation or policy positions aligned with Trump or Israeli government. BDS Target — Designated by USCPR, AFSC, or UN as complicit in Israeli occupation or military operations.

Data Sources

FEC / OpenSecrets · More Perfect Union · USCPR BDS Boycott List · AFSC Gaza Genocide Companies · Brennan Center · White House Ballroom Donors · Al Jazeera / Mondoweiss UN reports · CNBC inauguration donor reporting.

Update Schedule

An AI agent runs daily at 7:00 AM CT, searching FEC filings, NGO reports, and news sources for new incidents. All entries are scored and written to the database automatically. Historical incidents remain permanently logged.
